What is the Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the psychological process of bringing one's attention to experiences occurring in the present moment, which one can develop through the practice of meditation and through other training.



Positive People vs. Negative People

Positive People Negative People
They choose to focus on the bright side. They always complain.
They know when to let go. They refuse to focus on the present and themselves.
Lots of praise. Lots of criticism.
They see failure as a great opportunity to learn. They find it really hard to accept and admit failure.


Optimism

Optimism is a mental attitude reflecting a belief or hope that the outcome of some specific endeavor, or outcomes in general, will be positive, favorable, and desirable. A common idiom used to illustrate optimism versus pessimism is a glass filled with water to the halfway point: an optimist is said to see the glass as half full, while a pessimist sees the glass as half empty.
